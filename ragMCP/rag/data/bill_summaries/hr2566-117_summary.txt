
     <p><b>No TikTok on Government Devices Act</b></p> <p>This bill requires the social media video application TikTok to be removed from the information technology of federal agencies. </p> <p>Specifically, the bill requires the Office of Management and Budget to develop standards for executive agencies that require TikTok and any successor application from the developer to be removed from agency information technology (e.g., devices). Such standards must include exceptions for law enforcement activities, national security interests, and security researchers.</p>
